Philosophy

SCI*KE education provides an open forum for collaboration between science teachers, students and nature enthusiasts. It promotes a connection between the classroom and our environment. As a collegiate group, science teachers aim to provide professional development for each other.

"Meetings are held on the trails and ideas are inspired by nature!"

William's Warriors Foundation

Please bring a $10 donation for William’s Warriors, a nonprofit dedicated to raising money for pediatric brain cancer. The money raised by William’s Superhero Fund will support childhood cancer research in the laboratory of Robert Wechsler-Reya, Ph.D., SBP scientist and world-renowned expert on medulloblastoma.
